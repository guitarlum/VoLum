What changed in v5

This pass keeps the accepted v4 UX but tightens the visual language: quieter copy, cleaner cards, stronger baseline resemblance, and a more legible DELAY → REVERB signal flow. The bypass affordance still lives on each pedal card's LED, and AMP-only toggles stay hidden whenever POST is expanded.
