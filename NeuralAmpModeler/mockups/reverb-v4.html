How POST works in v4

POST is a pedalboard. Each effect is a self-contained pedal card in signal-flow order (NDSP-style). The focused card has an amber border + amber corner brackets + amber name; its controls fill the bottom knob row, and its preset name takes the amp-name slot beneath the pedalboard. Active-but-not-focused cards keep a teal border + teal corner brackets and a lit LED. Click anywhere on a card to focus it; click the LED in the bottom-right of a card to bypass that effect without losing focus. When PRE eventually ships its own effects (compressor, OD, etc.), it uses the same pedalboard pattern.
